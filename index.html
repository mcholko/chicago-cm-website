Chicago CM
Who We Are

Chicago's Premier Construction Professional Network

Chicago CM is a dedicated networking community for commercial construction professionals across the Chicagoland area. We connect the people who build this city — creating relationships that drive projects forward.

Genuine Connections
Curated events designed to foster real professional relationships, not just card exchanges.
Chicago-Focused
100% focused on the Chicagoland commercial market — the people, projects, and opportunities that matter here.
Industry-Specific
Not a general business network. Every member works in or supports commercial construction.
Membership

Built for the Entire Construction Ecosystem

Chicago CM is open to all commercial construction professionals. If you build Chicago, you belong here.

General Contractors
Connect with owners, design teams, and subcontractor partners. Expand your bid list and build the relationships that win work.
Architects & Designers
Meet the contractors and owners who bring your designs to life. Strengthen partnerships that make projects run smoothly.
Subcontractors
Get in front of the GCs and CMs who are always looking for reliable trade partners. Be top of mind when bids go out.
Owners Representatives
Vet and connect with contractors, consultants, and vendors who can deliver for your clients.
Construction Managers
Build your bench of trusted partners. Connect with owners, consultants, and trade contractors across every sector.
Owners & Developers
Find vetted, trusted construction partners and stay connected to market trends in Chicago's commercial real estate landscape.
